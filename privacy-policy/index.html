Privacy Policy — Last updated: April 29, 2026

ScanPrice is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Camera Permission

ScanPrice requests access to your device's camera solely to scan barcodes and QR codes for the purpose of looking up and comparing product prices.

  • The camera is only activated when you initiate a scan
  • No images or video are stored on your device by the app
  • No images or video are transmitted to any server or third party
  • The camera feed is processed locally on your device in real time

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account registration or login is required
  • No location data is collected
  • No device identifiers are stored or transmitted
  • No usage analytics or tracking is performed

Third-Party Services

ScanPrice does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Scanned barcodes may be used to query public product databases for price information; these queries do not include any personally identifiable information.

Children's Privacy

ScanPrice does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
